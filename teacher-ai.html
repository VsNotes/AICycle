🗺️ YOUR AI LEARNING ROADMAP
1
Use ChatGPT for Your Next Lesson (Week 1)

Ask it to generate 10 discussion questions on your current topic. Takes 30 seconds. Notice how many you'd use. That's your starting point.

2
Try an AI Marking Tool (Week 2–3)

MagicSchool AI, Khanmigo, or even ChatGPT with your rubric. Grade 5 essays and see the time saved. Keep the editorial control.

3
Build a Subject Resource Bot (Month 1)

Upload your scheme of work and key documents. Use a RAG setup (no coding) to let students query it. See the RAG tab to understand how.

4
Teach AI Literacy to Students (Month 2)

You don't need to be an expert — teach critical thinking about AI output. How to prompt well, how to verify facts, how to use it ethically.

5
Lead AI Policy in Your School (Month 3)

Educators who understand AI are shaping school policy. Be the voice for thoughtful, empowering AI use — not a blanket ban.
