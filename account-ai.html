Understanding AI (Foundation)
No tool yet — build the mental model first

Before touching any AI tool, you need 30 minutes to understand what AI actually is and — more importantly — what it gets wrong. AI is confident even when incorrect, so for finance work, you are always the final check.

📋 What to do
1
Open the INTRO and LLMs tabs on AICycle (top menu). Read both — 30 minutes total.
2
Note the difference between AI categorising data (very reliable) vs AI calculating/predicting (always verify).
3
Understand: AI never sees client data unless YOU upload it. This matters for confidentiality decisions in later chapters.
🎯 TRY THIS TODAYOpen ChatGPT (free) and ask: "Explain what a balance sheet is to someone with no finance background." Notice how clear and fast it is — that's the foundation skill for Chapter 4.

Dext (Receipt Bank)
AI-powered receipt and invoice capture
dext.com ↗

Dext uses AI to read photos of receipts and invoices — extracting supplier, date, amount, and VAT automatically, then publishing the data to Xero, QuickBooks, or Sage. Most firms already have access to this through their software bundle.

📋 How to set it up
1
Check if Dext is already included in your Xero/QuickBooks subscription — many plans bundle it free.
2
Install the Dext mobile app. Photograph a real receipt — AI extracts the data in seconds.
3
Set up the email forwarding address — forward supplier invoice emails directly, no photo needed.
4
Review the AI's "publish" suggestions for the first week before trusting auto-publish.
🎯 TRY THIS TODAYTake a photo of any receipt with the Dext app (free trial available) and watch it extract supplier, amount, and date automatically. Time it — compare to manual entry.
🔄
Xero (Bank Rules + AI Reconciliation)
Automated bank matching that learns patterns
xero.com ↗

Xero's AI-assisted reconciliation suggests matches between bank transactions and invoices, and learns from your corrections. Over time, "Find & Match" suggestions become highly accurate for repeat suppliers.

📋 How to use it
1
Go to Accounting → Bank Accounts → Reconcile. Xero shows AI-suggested matches on the right.
2
Create Bank Rules for recurring transactions (rent, subscriptions) — set conditions once, AI applies them forever.
3
Each time you confirm or correct a suggestion, the AI model improves for that client's patterns.
4
Use "Cash summary" reports — Xero's AI flags unusual spikes automatically.
🎯 TRY THIS TODAYOpen a client file → Reconcile tab. Count how many transactions already have a green "match found" suggestion. Confirm 5 of them and notice how fast it is.

ChatGPT (Client Communication)
Turn numbers into plain-English client updates
chat.openai.com ↗

ChatGPT's free tier is enough for this. The skill is prompting — giving it your numbers/notes and asking for a specific output format (email, summary, explanation).

📋 How to use it
1
Create a free account at chat.openai.com (no card needed for free tier).
2
Never paste client names or identifying details — use placeholders like "[Client A]" or round figures.
3
Use this exact prompt structure: "Turn these notes into a [professional email / simple explanation / summary]: [your notes]"
4
Always re-read the output — check no numbers were changed or invented by the AI.
🎯 TRY THIS TODAYPaste this into ChatGPT: "Turn these rough notes into a professional client email: Revenue up 12% this quarter, expenses up 8% due to new hire, cash position healthy, VAT return due 15th." — then personalise the result.

RAG — Build a Tax Knowledge Bot
AI that answers from YOUR documents, not the internet
AICycle RAG ↗

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) means: upload your firm's tax guidance docs → AI searches THOSE docs first → answers based only on what's there. This avoids generic/outdated answers and keeps answers grounded in your actual rules.

📋 Easiest starting point (no coding)
1
Try Notion AI or Microsoft Copilot — both let you "ask questions" about uploaded documents/pages already.
2
Upload last year's HMRC/IRS guidance PDF + your firm's internal policy doc into Notion.
3
Ask: "According to these documents, what is the VAT threshold?" — it answers FROM your file, with a citation.
4
For a firm-wide bot, read the full RAG tab on AICycle — it explains the technical version for IT/dev teams to build.
🎯 TRY THIS TODAYOpen Microsoft Copilot or Notion AI → upload one PDF (any tax guidance doc) → ask it a specific question about that document. See how it answers from the file, not generic training data.
