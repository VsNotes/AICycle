😨 MYTHS vs REALITY
😰AI will automate the Scrum Master roleFEAR
Agile is fundamentally about people, trust, and continuous improvement. No AI can facilitate a difficult retrospective, coach a dysfunctional team, or build psychological safety. Human judgment is the core of the role.
😤AI tools don't understand AgileFEAR
Modern LLMs are trained on the Scrum Guide, SAFe, Kanban, and thousands of Agile case studies. They can generate user stories, identify impediments, suggest retrospective formats, and analyse sprint metrics fluently.
✅AI frees you for high-value coachingFACT
Scrum Masters spend huge time on status updates, Jira grooming, and meeting notes. AI handles all of that, giving you more time for team coaching, stakeholder management, and process improvement.
